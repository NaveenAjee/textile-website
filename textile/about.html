Our Story

Crafting premium textiles with passion and precision since 1985

Our History

TextileCo was founded in 1985 by textile engineer James Wilson with a simple mission: to create fabrics that combine beauty, durability, and sustainability. What began as a small workshop with just five employees has grown into a global textile manufacturer with facilities across three continents.

Through decades of innovation and dedication to quality, we've established ourselves as a trusted partner for fashion designers, interior decorators, and industrial clients worldwide. Our commitment to ethical manufacturing and sustainable practices has been at the heart of our business from day one.

Today, TextileCo continues to be family-owned and operated, with the second generation of the Wilson family leading our company into a future where textiles play an increasingly important role in both everyday life and specialized applications.

Our Manufacturing Process

From raw materials to finished products, we maintain the highest standards at every step.

Spinning and Weaving
